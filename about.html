St. Columba's College ...

One of the oldest colleges with provision for higher education in Jharkhand and across eastern India, St.Columba’s College, Hazaribagh owes its name to the esteemed Irish Saint Columba. St.Columba’s College is accredited by UGC and has been affiliated to the Vinoba Bhave University since 1992. The institution outreaches on a public domain and offers every fortune to the students of major disciplines of Arts and Science. Dispersed over a sweeping area of 23 acre campus, the college hosts a multifarious range of facilities to provide ample aid in delivering quality and efficacy and undoubtedly flatters itself to be one of the best colleges in Jharkhand.

Students desiring to be future ready definitely have to look for not one, but a whole gamut of conditions to pave their way to a notable as well as exemplary career path. St.Columba’s College delineates much higher standards and can pretty easily suffice you check boxes for requisites while you are planning to take admission for undergraduate or postgraduate courses. The admission process to the college can be either merit based or through entrance exam followed by personal interview. Presently the college offers admission to more than 15 courses. Academic sessions complemented by extra curricular ventures tend to build your persona dynamically and deem you fit for a righteous place under the sun.

A seamlessly tranquil atmosphere prevails across the campus while surprising with optimal hustle bustle during active sessions. One of the best colleges in the state stands erect with intricately designed buildings representing the architectural style of the then late nineteenth century. Lush campus backed up by adequately defined infrastructure and facilities enshrine the right of every student to freely acquire the theories and practical skill sets. Be it library, well equipped science laboratories, playground, computer labs or hostel accommodation, St.Columba’s College proves its mettle of being stationed as one of the top academic institutions in Jharkhand. The college may flaunt with pride the overt participation of students in extracurricular activities and bagging numerous prizes and appreciations.
